Connecting Elements – The Nobel Prize in Chemistry

Connecting Elements is a museum exhibition that displays the work of Nobel Laureates in Chemistry.
Through interactive themes, visitors learn how elements and molecules play a role in creating the world around us, and how chemical reactions can help to consciously shape the future in our planet.
